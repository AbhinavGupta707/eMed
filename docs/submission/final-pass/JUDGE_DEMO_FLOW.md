# Judge-ready final-pass demo flow

## Three-minute primary flow

| Time | Operator action                                       | Judge-facing line                                                                 |
| ---: | ----------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 0:00 | Open the proactive invitation                         | “HomeRounds starts from a bounded synthetic change, not continuous monitoring.”   |
| 0:20 | Begin the Maya check-in and use voice fixture or type | “Voice can propose fields, but Maya reviews every field; typing has full parity.” |
| 0:50 | Confirm the structured report                         | “The model cannot advance safety, quality, protocol, or action state.”            |
| 1:05 | Show the single adaptive station                      | “Only one useful check is selected. Consent and quality still gate any result.”   |
| 1:25 | Use phone result or honest unavailable path           | “An uncertain or failed capture never becomes a number.”                          |
| 1:45 | Show baseline/memory and explicit action review       | “Memory is structured, consented, correctable, and has no action authority.”      |
| 2:10 | Confirm the synthetic care-team action                | “This is saved inside HomeRounds only; no clinic or external service is claimed.” |
| 2:30 | Open clinician cockpit, complete, return to patient   | “Ownership, contact, completion, and patient status share one audit trail.”       |
| 2:55 | Point to the boundary                                 | “Synthetic prototype, not medical care, and not clinically validated.”            |

## Safety insert

If asked about emergency behavior, use the red-flag fixture. Show the hard stop before sensing, the absence of a number, and the statement that HomeRounds cannot assess an emergency or contact an emergency service.

## Operator fallbacks

- Voice unavailable: switch immediately to text; do not troubleshoot a live provider during judging.
- Camera/microphone denied or unsupported: show the explicit unavailable state and continue without a measurement.
- Poor signal: show retry/quality explanation; use the labelled sample only when its explicit control appears and call it a labelled, non-live sample.
- Fireworks slow/failure/abstain: show the deterministic usual route and state that the model did not gain authority.
- QR expired/disconnected: reissue, reconnect, or continue on the computer. Never reuse a consumed fragment.
- Hosted Preview unavailable: use the exact local production-build candidate and say it is local. Do not substitute the historical Checkpoint 10 URL as Checkpoint 11 evidence.
- Clinician mutation stale: reload the queue and retry with the current version; do not edit storage.

## Claims to avoid

Do not say physical iPhone-tested, Windows-tested, clinically accurate, medically validated, emergency-connected, continuously monitoring, sent to a real clinic/pharmacy/care team, production-ready, or deployed as Checkpoint 11 until the corresponding closure record exists. Emulated Windows Chrome and iPhone 12 contexts must always be described as emulated.
