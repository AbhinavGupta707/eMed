## Provider release position

The demonstrable release profile is no-key: `VOICE_PROVIDER=disabled`, `NARRATIVE_MODEL_PROVIDER=disabled`, and `OPTICAL_ASSESSMENT_PROVIDER=finger_ppg`. Until `W-02` closes, the operator must not claim a live optical success. The allowed recovery sequence is one coached attempt when appropriate, an explicitly selected and visibly labelled recorded-synthetic replay only after failure, then the poor-quality/no-measurement scenario. VitalLens must remain unavailable unless `W-03` and the physical-device portions of `W-02` close.
